Introduction

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Welcome to Water Tank, developed by Vision Co. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our application operates. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and your rights as a user.

By using our application, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

01 — Data Collection

Information We Do Not Collect

Water Tank is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your device. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Photos, contacts, or media from your device
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Financial or payment information

All data you enter within the app remains entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

02 — Third-Party Services

External Services & Links

Our app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. Any external links within the app lead to vendor or service provider websites that have their own independent privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies before sharing any information with those services.

03 — Children's Privacy

Users Under 13

Water Tank is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the app in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us immediately.

04 — Data Security

How Your Data is Protected

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted by our app, there is no risk of your information being accessed, disclosed, or misused through our systems. Any content you create or save in the app is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's own security measures.

05 — Policy Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
